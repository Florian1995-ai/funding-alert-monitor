## Key patterns and strategic notes

**Chocolate makers are the highest-value targets.** Stores 1, 3, 4, 5, 13, 20, 21, 22, 31, 32, and the bonus Mendocino Chocolate Company are all potential **ingredient buyers** for Peruvian Cacao — not just retailers. A B2B ingredient sale to a chocolatier is recurring revenue and higher volume than shelf placement.

**Co-ops have the most structured onboarding.** Berryvale Grocery (#39) has a downloadable vendor application. Quincy/Feather River Co-ops (#43-44) are NCG members with established distribution channels. These are the most predictable path to getting on shelves.

**Apple Hill stores offer seasonal volume spikes.** The Apple Hill corridor (Stores 32-37) draws **500,000+ visitors annually** from September through December. Seasonal placement in these stores provides massive exposure even if year-round sales are lower.

**The Peruvian connection is an underused selling point.** Pachamama Coffee (#29) already sources from Peru and uses the Quechua word for "Mother Earth" as its name. The New Incan Empire brand story and Pachamama's mission create a natural co-marketing opportunity that goes beyond a standard wholesale relationship.

**Owner contact information was hardest to find for ethnic grocery stores and very small shops.** Stores like Mercado Loco, Punjab Bazaar, Masala Market, Four Seasons Market, and Gastronom Kiev Market have minimal online presence. These are best approached in person with product samples rather than via email or social media outreach. California Secretary of State business filings (bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov) can reveal LLC member names for stores where owner identity was not publicly available.

**LinkedIn is not widely used by small-town food shop owners.** Only three LinkedIn profiles were found across all 50 stores: Bill Hansen (Nevada City Chocolate Shoppe), Sabrina Doyle (Berryvale Grocery GM), and the Berryvale company page. Facebook and Instagram are the primary social channels for these businesses. Direct phone calls and in-person visits with samples will be far more effective than digital outreach for the majority of these prospects.
