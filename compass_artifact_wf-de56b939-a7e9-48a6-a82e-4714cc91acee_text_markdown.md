# Northern California specialty food store prospecting guide for New Incan Empire

**New Incan Empire's Peruvian Pink Salt (Sal de Maras) and Peruvian Cacao have over 50 viable retail prospects across Northern California**, with the strongest cluster right in the Nevada City–Grass Valley corridor. The region's health-conscious, affluent, artisan-oriented consumer base creates ideal conditions for premium Peruvian imports. Several stores already carry specialty salts, international gourmet ingredients, or South American products — making them ready for immediate outreach.

Each store below is scored on a **Priority scale of 1–5** (5 = highest fit), factoring in product alignment, price positioning, purchasing accessibility, and geographic proximity to Rough and Ready.

---
