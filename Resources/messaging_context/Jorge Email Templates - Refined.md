# Jorge's Refined Email Templates

Based on:
- Sandi Victoria's high-converting cold email copy (42% open, 34% CTR)
- ELITIFY video walkthrough on personalization and frictionless conversion
- Jorge's ICP: Recently funded startups needing tech teams fast

---

## Key Principles Applied

### From Sandi's Copy:
1. **"Re:" subject line** - Looks like a reply, not cold email
2. **Trigger event opener** - Reference specific funding announcement
3. **"Quick question" hook** - Low friction engagement
4. **Insider knowledge** - "Most founders don't know..."
5. **Specific numbers** - 72 hours, 14 days, exact case study metrics
6. **"Based on your [X]" personalization** - Calculated estimate from THEIR data
7. **P.S. objection handler** - Address "but we already have devs" concern

### From ELITIFY Video:
1. **Pre-research every prospect** - Make it feel 1-to-1
2. **Frictionless CTA** - Calendar link, no back-and-forth
3. **Follow-up sequence** - Automated if no reply
4. **Retargeting** - Different angles in follow-ups

## METRICS TO TRACK

Based on ELITIFY's results (42% open, 34% CTR):

- **Target open rate:** 35-45%
- **Target click rate:** 20-35%
- **Target reply rate:** 5-10%
- **Target meeting rate:** 2-5%

If below these, adjust:
- Subject line (if opens low)
- Body copy/CTA (if clicks low)
- Offer/positioning (if replies low)
